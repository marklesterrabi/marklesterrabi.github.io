Visual Recognition Guide

How to identify your corn seeds:

🌽 Waxy Corn

Pearlescent white/pale yellow kernels, waxy appearance, chewy texture

🌽 Sweet Corn

Bright golden-yellow, plump kernels, slight translucency, sweet aroma

🌽 Hybrid Yellow

Deep yellow/orange, uniform size, robust texture, dense kernel fill

Tip: Place seeds on white background with good lighting for best recognition results!
